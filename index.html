Fun Facts About Dogs

Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell, with 220 million scent receptors in their noses, compared to only 5-6 million in humans.

The Basenji is the only dog breed that cannot bark.

The world's oldest known breed is the Saluki, originally bred in ancient Egypt.
